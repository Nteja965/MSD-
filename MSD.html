MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand, is a legendary Indian cricketer. Known for his cool-headed leadership, Dhoni captained India to major victories, including the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy. He's also a star in the IPL, leading the Chennai Super Kings to multiple titles. Famous for his finishing skills and the iconic helicopter shot, Dhoni has a fanatical following. Beyond cricket, he is a motorcycle enthusiast and remains admired for his humility and impact on Indian cricket. A true icon in every sense.
